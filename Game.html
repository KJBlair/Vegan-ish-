Play your carbon right game

To play, click on any of the non-vegan items in the fridge to see their carbon footprints and find easy vegan replacements.

Chicken

Chicken has a carbon footprint of 6.9kg CO2 per kg, equivalent of 16 miles in car. A tasty replacement for chicken in a meal can be vegan Quorn chicken pieces. They are a lower fat and higher fiber option.

Bacon

Bacon has a carbon footprint of 12.1kg CO2 per kg, equivalent of 28 miles in car. Instead of bacon why not try Tempeh - a tasty, fermented soybean product. Or some shitake mushrooms or seitan, which can have the tough, chewiness of bacon.

Sausage

Sausage has a carbon footprint of 12.1kg CO2 per kg, equivalent of 28 miles in car. Tofu, mushrooms and tempeh all provide tasty vegan replacements for sausages, however, Linda McCartney and Quorn also offer some exciting alternatives.

Fish

Fish has a carbon footprint of 6.1kg CO2 per kg, equivalent to 14 miles in car. Why not try Quorn Vegan Fishless Fingers instead?

Milk

Milk has a carbon footprint of 1.9kg CO2 per kg, equivalent of 4 miles in a car. Why not try plant based alternatives like soya, oat or almond milk?
