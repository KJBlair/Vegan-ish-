The KFC burger

by Lucy and Kirsty

Posted on March 24, 2020 at 12:00 PM

Trying to keep up with the likes of wagamama and greggs, KFC have knuckled down to create their new plant based delight.

While vegans and vegetarians alike may be disappointed that the burger cannot be enjoyed with vegan chips :( the burger itself is a revelation. Extremely well seasoned with its own vegan mayo, the burger was a tasty treat. However, we have to ask ourselves whether it can sit in the god tier of the greggs vegan sausage roll?

"The burger is a revelation."

Lucy and Kirsty

And value for money with satisfying hunger cravings we have to say no. Therefore we give the vegan KFC burger 8/10.


Greggs Vegan Bake

by Lucy and Kirsty

Posted on March 24, 2020 at 12:00 PM

As one of the pioneers of vegan fast food, Greggs will always have a special place in our hearts.

Last year, the northern power house produced the 10/10 culinary sensation: the vegan sausage roll, which quickly became a staple for vegans and omnivores alike. This year brought the vegan bake and as gravy lovers, boy were we excited.

"Greggs will always have a special place in our hearts."

Lucy and Kirsty

The vegan bake is very much a homage to the beloved Greggs steak bake, but entirely vegan friendly and while it does offer a tasty change to the Greggs sausage roll, we weren't sure it quite lived up to it's older pastry brother. Therefore we give the Greggs vegan bake 7/10.
